Residential Development

Waverly is growing — and we’re welcoming responsible residential development that enhances our community, meets the needs of families, and supports a strong local economy. Whether you're a homebuilder, land developer, or resident, Waverly offers opportunities for smart growth and quality living.

Residential Growth Goals

  • Support mixed-use and diverse housing types to meet all stages of life.
  • Encourage development near schools, parks, and public infrastructure.
  • Collaborate with developers on zoning and permitting processes.
  • Preserve small-town charm while supporting modern living standards.

New Neighborhoods

We're planning for growth with thoughtfully designed subdivisions and amenities.

Diverse Housing Options

Waverly encourages a mix of single-family homes, apartments, and townhouses.

Community-Focused

Residential growth is guided by our commitment to walkability and green spaces.
