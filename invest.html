Investment Attraction

Waverly is committed to attracting sustainable investments that align with our community values and long-term vision. We create a welcoming environment for investors by offering key incentives, ready infrastructure, and a streamlined development process.

Why Invest in Waverly?

  • Strategic location with access to major highways and regional markets.
  • Development-ready sites with infrastructure in place.
  • Tax increment financing (TIF) and other incentive programs.
  • Supportive local government and expedited permitting.

Prime Development Sites

We offer shovel-ready locations ideal for commercial, retail, or mixed-use projects.

Incentive Programs

Investors may qualify for financial tools like TIF, tax relief, or state grants.

Strong Partnerships

We collaborate with businesses and developers to ensure long-term project success.
