Our Mission

The Waverly Economic Development Corporation (WEDC) of Waverly Nebraska is committed to fostering sustainable economic growth, attracting investment, and supporting local businesses through strategic initiatives and partnerships.

We focus on creating opportunities for local entrepreneurs, supporting workforce development, and ensuring a vibrant, thriving community for residents and businesses alike.

Growth Highlights

  • 4,500 Waverly Population
  • 345,357 20 Mile Radius Population
  • 529,574 30 Mile Radius Population
  • 53,995 Annual Average Daily Traffic
  • 50+ Local Businesses Supported
  • 10M+ Investment Secured (Past 5 Years)
  • 20% Increase in Local Jobs Since 2020
  • 8 Strategic Initiatives Annually

Our Goals

  • Attract and retain diverse industries to strengthen the local economy.
  • Support workforce development through education and training partnerships.
  • Promote sustainable growth and community-focused development.
  • Enhance quality of life for residents through infrastructure and innovation.
