About Us

The Economic Development Committee of the City of Waverly is committed to fostering sustainable economic growth, attracting investment, and supporting local businesses through strategic initiatives and partnerships.

Our Strategic Goals

Business Retention

We support local businesses with resources and incentives to grow and thrive.

Investment Attraction

We promote Waverly as a destination for commercial and sustainable investment.

Workforce Development

We work with educational institutions to build a skilled and future-ready workforce.

Business Opportunities

Real Estate

Explore commercial sites and spaces ready for development.

Startup Support

Incentives and programs for entrepreneurs and small businesses.

Green Initiatives

Partner with us on eco-friendly projects that benefit the community.
