Sponsors & Partnerships

The City of Waverly is proud to collaborate with a diverse group of sponsors and community partners who support our mission to build a vibrant, inclusive economy. These organizations contribute resources, expertise, and shared values to help us achieve lasting impact.

Featured Sponsors

Waverly Community Bank

Waverly Bank funds economic development programs and helps local entrepreneurs grow their businesses.

Civic Energy Corp

Supporting our sustainability goals, Civic Energy sponsors public energy education and clean power projects.

MediaWorks

MediaWorks amplifies our outreach through generous marketing support across digital and print channels.

Featured Partners

TechCo Solutions

Partnering on smart city initiatives, TechCo enables innovation in connectivity and data infrastructure.

GreenLife Alliance

GreenLife collaborates on sustainability campaigns, urban gardening, and community education projects.

Waverly Chamber of Commerce

A key partner in business retention and networking, the Chamber supports economic outreach initiatives.
