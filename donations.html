Support Waverly's Growth

The City of Waverly Economic Development Corporation appreciates the generosity of community members, businesses, and partners who support our initiatives.

How to Donate

Your contributions help us foster economic growth, support local businesses, and create opportunities for all residents.

  • Online: Donate securely via our online donation portal.
  • By Mail: Send checks payable to City of Waverly Economic Development to:
    14130 Lancashire Street,
    Waverly, NE 68462
  • In Person: Visit our office during business hours to donate or learn more.

Tax Information

All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Please consult your tax advisor for details.
