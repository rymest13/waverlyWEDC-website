Business Retention

The City of Waverly is dedicated to supporting local businesses as the backbone of our economy. Our business retention strategy is focused on building long-term relationships, removing barriers to growth, and fostering a strong local ecosystem.

Our Approach

  • Conducting regular outreach and listening sessions with business owners.
  • Helping businesses navigate regulations and permitting efficiently.
  • Providing access to financial incentives and growth programs.
  • Offering training and networking opportunities through local partnerships.

On-the-Ground Support

Our team meets with business leaders to understand their needs and help them grow locally.

Tools & Resources

We provide programs, grants, and tax relief options that directly support business retention.

Community Collaboration

We facilitate strong connections between local businesses, government, and education partners.
